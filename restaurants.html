COQBULL


Coqbull is a Chicken and Burger restaurant found in 5th French Church, Cork City.


Dont let that you keep you away from their Delicious donuts with a side of any type of chocolate you could imagine!


For me, this is a need to-go place the food is amazing and staff are welcoming.


Its as close to the center of the city wihtout the noise of the city.

Lab 82


Lab 82 is a beauitful diner on 82 Lower Glanmire Rd, Victorian Quarter where you can go and have Pancakes, Burgers Brownies and Brunch.


The desserts and breakfast here are to die for! Staff are unbeliveably kind, food ready very quick if you are in a rush and no need to reserve a chair for yourself as its all walk-ins!


The only problem i can find with this place is the opening hours.


I can honestly say i have never been disappointed while eating here.

Tequila Jacks


Tequila Jacks is located in City Quarter, Lapp's Quay and is one of the most unique restaurants I have ever seen with excellent Mexican food served.


What makes Tequila Jacks so unique is a dome outside their main restaurant, right beside the river. This makes the experience so much more different then your usual dinner with great food to add to it!


In my opinion, this is a must for people who are here to try something creative and new.
