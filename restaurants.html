Coqbull is a Chicken and Burger restaurant found in 5th French Church, Cork City.

For me, this is a need to-go place the food is amazing and staff are welcoming.

Lab 82


Lab 82 is a beauitful diner on 82 Lower Glanmire Rd, Victorian Quarter where you can go and have Pancakes, Burgers Brownies and Brunch.


The desserts and breakfast here are to die for! Staff are unbeliveably kind, food ready very quick if you are in a rush and no need to reserve a chair for yourself as its all walk-ins!


The only problem i can find with this place is the opening hours.


I can honestly say i have never been disappointed while eating here.
